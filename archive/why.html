Why Recipe exists

Four questions. Honest answers. If the answer is "use something else," we say so.

1. What does this actually replace?

The repeat AI calls in your product where the answer is small and structured: yes/no, pick a category, extract these tokens.

2. When should you NOT use Recipe?

We are not the answer to every problem. Use the right tool:

  • Open-ended generation. "Write a marketing email" — not us, use Claude / GPT.
  • Long-context reasoning. "Read this 80-page PDF and extract the contract clauses" — use a model with long context.
  • Multi-step agent loops. Browse the web, plan, take actions — use Claude Code, OpenAI Assistants, or a real agent.
  • Domain knowledge you don't have examples for. "Is this molecule toxic?" with no labeled data — you need a real classifier with real training.
  • Recall over a corpus. "Find me prior conversations with this customer" — that's Memory, not Skills.
  • Tasks where the right answer changes with world state. "Is this stock a buy?" — not a fixed recipe; that's an inference problem with fresh data.
