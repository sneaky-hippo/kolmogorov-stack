HEDIS BCS — Breast Cancer Screening

A kolm template that computes the HEDIS Breast Cancer Screening (BCS) measure: the percentage of women 50-74 who had at least one mammogram (CPT 77065, 77066, 77067, HCPCS G0202) within the 27-month measurement period (24 months + 3-month look-back). Class: synthesized-rule (class 1).

recipe-class: synthesized-rule (class 1) spec: NCQA HEDIS MY 2024+ (BCS) period: 27 months (24 + 3 look-back)

Input & output spec

Input is a FHIR R4 Bundle containing the female member roster ages 50-74 and their Procedure resources for the 27-month measurement period. Output is a single measure record with the rate and a per-member array. Members with bilateral mastectomy (ICD-10 Z90.13 or any of CPT 19303-19307) are excluded from the denominator.

{
  "resourceType": "Bundle",

        "id":        "pt-1",

Qualifying mammography codes

A screening counts toward the numerator if it is documented as one of these real CPT or HCPCS codes within the 27-month window. CPT 77067 is screening mammography (bilateral); 77065 and 77066 are diagnostic; G0202 is the HCPCS code for digital screening mammography.

CPT 77065
diagnostic, unilateral
CPT 77066
diagnostic, bilateral
CPT 77067
screening, bilateral
HCPCS G0202
screening digital mammography
ICD-10 Z90.13
exclusion: bilateral mastectomy
CPT 19303-19307
exclusion: mastectomy procedures

Continuous enrollment is required with no gap exceeding 45 days during the 24-month measurement period. The 3-month look-back lets a screening from the prior calendar quarter count toward the current report.

4-ring receipt schema

Every measure computation ships a receipt your auditor can re-verify offline. The chain proves which bundle was ingested, which version of the rule library was applied, the numerator and denominator that resulted, and the seal.

ring 01 · pre-image
bundle_hash + member_count + measurement_period_27mo
tenant-rotated HMAC
ring 02 · derived
rule_library_version + cpt_hcpcs_table_hash
bound to recipe_version
ring 03 · execution
numerator + denominator + per_member_digest
deterministic, no judge
ring 04 · seal
SHA-256(ring1..3) · verify endpoint
offline-verifiable

Verify command: kolm verify --hash <receipt_hash>. Runs locally against the seal, no network required.
