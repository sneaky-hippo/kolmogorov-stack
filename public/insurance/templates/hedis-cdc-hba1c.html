HEDIS HbA1c — Diabetes HbA1c Poor Control (> 9.0%)

A kolm template that computes the HEDIS Glycemic Status Assessment for Patients with Diabetes (GSD) measure, specifically the HbA1c poor-control rate (members 18-75 with diabetes whose most recent HbA1c in the measurement year was > 9.0% or missing). Lower rate is better. Class: synthesized-rule (class 1).

recipe-class: synthesized-rule (class 1) spec: NCQA HEDIS MY 2024+ (GSD) direction: inverted (lower is better)

Input & output spec

Input is a FHIR R4 Bundle containing the qualifying diabetic member roster and their HbA1c Observation resources for the measurement year. Output is a single measure record. The denominator is members 18-75 with a diabetes ICD-10 code; the numerator is members whose most recent HbA1c reading was > 9.0% OR no HbA1c was documented at all.

Numerator logic

Three conditions promote a member into the poor-control numerator. The threshold and the missing-data treatment are pinned in the pack rules so QA can re-derive any per-member flag without ambiguity.

most recent HbA1c
> 9.0%
no HbA1c documented
counted as poor control
age range
18-75 with diabetes
Inverted measure: for the HbA1c poor-control sub-measure, a lower rate is better. Plan reporting often inverts this to "HbA1c control" (1 - rate) for board-facing dashboards. The artifact reports the raw poor-control rate so downstream code does not branch on direction without the manifest.

4-ring receipt schema

Every measure computation ships a receipt your auditor can re-verify offline. The chain proves which bundle was ingested, which version of the rule library was applied, the numerator and denominator that resulted, and the seal.

ring 01 · pre-image
bundle_hash + member_count + measurement_period
tenant-rotated HMAC
ring 02 · derived
rule_library_version + diabetes_code_table_hash
bound to recipe_version
ring 03 · execution
numerator + denominator + per_member_digest
deterministic, no judge
ring 04 · seal
SHA-256(ring1..3) · verify endpoint
offline-verifiable

Verify command: kolm verify --hash <receipt_hash>. Runs locally against the seal, no network required.
