01The pain

Why your ATO package
does not allow cloud LLMs.

Three concrete blockers from prime and program-office security architects.

ITAR

ITAR-controlled tech data cannot leave the US.

22 CFR 120-130 makes a frontier-API prompt against ITAR data a potential export. kolm runs inside your IL-5 enclave. No data leaves the boundary.

CUI / CMMC

CUI handling under CMMC 2.0 L2 forbids unmanaged cloud.

NIST 800-171 controls are inheritable only from authorized environments. kolm ships as a signed artifact runnable in your existing IL-4 or IL-5 enclave.

Air gap

Tactical edge has no link to the cloud.

Deployed cells run disconnected for hours or weeks. kolm artifacts are local-only at runtime; capture happens before deployment and ships in the same image.
