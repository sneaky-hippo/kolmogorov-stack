kolm.ai vs Kolm therapeutics: brand disambiguation for procurement

A buyer searching "Kolm" lands on at least five different entities. None of the others are us; we are not any of them. This page is the procurement-grade reference so the contract goes to the right Kolm. Each card names sector, founder, founding year, and URL.

The five entities most often confused

YOU ARE HERE

kolm.ai · the AI compiler

Sector: Developer tools, AI infrastructure. Product: An AI compiler that turns captured prompts into a portable, signed, audit-ready model artifact (.kolm). URL: kolm.ai. Legal entity: Kolm Stack, Inc. Name origin: derived from Andrey Kolm, after whom Kolm complexity (the formal notion of the shortest description that produces an output) is named. The name is a nod to compiling a model that is the smallest faithful representation of a workload.

DIFFERENT COMPANY

Kolm therapeutics

Sector: Biotechnology. Focus: Therapeutic development; named entries in clinical-trial registries are the canonical reference. Why confused: Both are technology companies named Kolm. The name overlap is coincidental; there is no legal, financial, or operational relationship between Kolm therapeutics and kolm.ai. If you meant Kolm therapeutics: their website is the authoritative reference; we cannot route you there from this page.

DIFFERENT ENTITY

Kolm (the band)

Sector: Music. Region: European indie. Founders: the band. Why confused: SEO traffic for the bare word "kolm" historically lands on the band's Spotify and Bandcamp pages. Reference disambiguator: the band's discography is on streaming services under the artist name "Kolm"; our company is on the public web at kolm.ai.

DIFFERENT PERSON

Petter Kolm

Role: Professor of mathematical finance, NYU Courant Institute. Field: Quantitative finance, portfolio optimization. Why confused: Petter Kolm is an academic with a substantial publication record; searches for "Kolm" + finance / math return his papers. Disambiguator: Professor Kolm publishes under "Petter N. Kolm" with NYU affiliation; kolm.ai is a company with no academic or financial-research practice.

DIFFERENT INDUSTRY

Kolm engines

Sector: Internal-combustion piston engines, often for general aviation, marine, or specialty applications. Why confused: "Kolm" search results for engineering and mechanical contexts surface the engines manufacturer. Disambiguator: Kolm engines is a manufacturer of physical machinery; kolm.ai is a software company that ships a build tool and an artifact format.

How to be sure you are contracting with kolm.ai

  • Domain: the only legitimate kolm.ai domain is kolm.ai. Subdomains used: app.kolm.ai, docs.kolm.ai. We do not operate kolm.com, kolm.io, kolm.co, or any other TLD variant.
  • Legal entity: Kolm Stack, Inc.
  • Product surface: the .kolm artifact format (RS-1 spec), the kolm CLI, the kolm.ai web product. If the proposal references any of those by name, it is us.
  • BAA: our standard BAA is offered for the capture-window phase of HIPAA-regulated workloads. See /baa.
  • Procurement contact: via the /enterprise form. Email replies originate from a kolm.ai domain.

How to be sure you are not contracting with us when you meant someone else

If your procurement need is therapeutics, music, finance research, or aviation engines, you are not in the right place. The cards above name the canonical disambiguator for each entity. If the use case is "we need a vendor for [biotech / clinical trials / drug discovery]", you are looking for Kolm therapeutics (or a different vendor entirely); kolm.ai will not sign that contract because the scope does not match our product.

SEO note for procurement researchers

Search engines collapse "kolm" across all five entities above. The disambiguator that consistently resolves to us is "kolm.ai" (with the TLD). Adding "AI compiler", "Kolm Stack", or ".kolm artifact" to your search will return our pages and not the others. The brand-disambig sweep on every kolm.ai page names this entity in the first 100 words of body text precisely so search engines learn the disambiguation; the cards above are the canonical mapping.

Notes for legal review

For counsel reviewing whether an existing trademark or registered mark conflicts with this product: kolm.ai operates under the trade name kolm.ai, and the legal entity is Kolm Stack, Inc. The product category we operate in is "developer tools, AI infrastructure, build pipelines, compilers". We do not operate in biotech, therapeutics, pharmaceuticals, clinical trials, music recording, music publishing, internal-combustion engines, aerospace, or academic finance research. None of the other Kolm entities listed above operate in our category. If a buyer's vendor-management system flags a name collision, the disambiguating fields below are sufficient to route the contract correctly.

  • D-U-N-S, EIN, and business registration: provided on request through the /enterprise contact form for procurement onboarding.
  • Domain ownership of kolm.ai: verifiable via WHOIS; the registrant is Kolm Stack, Inc.
  • Product trademark status: the .kolm artifact format and the kolm CLI are products of Kolm Stack, Inc.
  • BAA + DPA: available for the capture-window phase of HIPAA-regulated workloads; see /baa. We do not offer a BAA for any biotech, clinical, or therapeutic use case because that is not our product.
  • Procurement reference number: issued per-engagement from our enterprise portal; quote it on any purchase order to avoid routing the contract to a different Kolm entity by accident.
