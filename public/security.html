01Architecture · data flow

Where data lives.
Where it doesn't.

The compile pipeline runs in our cloud (or your VPC, on Enterprise). The runtime runs on your users' device. After compile, the only thing that crosses our boundary is the manifest hash — never the artifact, never the corpus, never the receipts.

02Three layers of provenance

Signed at compile.
Anchored in the registry. Optionally on-chain.

Each layer is independent. You can verify the artifact without us, the registry without us, and the chain without us. Tampering at any layer is detectable.

Layer 01

Artifact signature.

HMAC-SHA-256 over every component (model, LoRA, recipes, index, tests, manifest). The signature is part of the file. Modify any byte and verification fails offline, immediately.

$ kolm verify support-triage.kolm
✓ manifest        sha256 7c34…a812
✓ model.gguf      sha256 e421…8be0
✓ lora.bin        sha256 f105…2c33
✓ recipes.json    sha256 3a9c…d014
✓ chain           valid
Layer 02

Registry anchor.

Every published artifact's manifest hash is recorded in the public Kolmogorov registry, signed by us. You verify which version was actually compiled. The registry is append-only.

$ kolm anchor support-triage.kolm
› manifest sha256 7c34…a812
› anchored 2026-05-07T14:22:31Z
› signed   kolmogorov-registry-2026
✓ registry   match
Layer 03

On-chain anchor.

Enterprise tier writes the manifest hash to Bitcoin OP_RETURN or Arweave on a configurable cadence. Useful when chain of custody must survive both us and the registry going away.

$ kolm anchor --on-chain
› chain    bitcoin
› tx       a4f1…3c92
› block    871,422
› fee      $0.18
✓ permanent   written

05Disclosure

Found a flaw?
Tell us first.

We run a continuous responsible-disclosure program. Real findings get acknowledged within 24 hours and resolved on a fixed schedule. We pay for findings that materially affect security.

Scope: kolm.ai, the compile pipeline, the public registry, the runtime, the SDK, the CLI, RS-1 spec implementations.

Out of scope: social engineering, denial of service, spam, third-party services we don't control.

Mail security@kolm.ai. PGP-encrypt anything sensitive.
