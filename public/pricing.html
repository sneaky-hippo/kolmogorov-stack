-What graduates you

The reasons people move tiers
are small in number.

Our pricing exists to track three real thresholds: the moment a hobby project becomes a workflow, the moment a workflow becomes a product, and the moment a product becomes a fleet. Pick the tier that matches today's threshold; switch when one of these is true.

Developer → Starter
When the artifact stops being optional.
  • You compiled the same task more than 3 times this month.
  • You want a private namespace, not a public-atlas pull.
  • You started caring whether someone else can read your prompt.
Starter → Pro
When users depend on the output.
  • You're shipping the artifact inside a customer-facing product.
  • You want CI/CD compiles on every PR.
  • You need a 24-hour SLA on Slack, not a forum thread.
Pro → Teams
When a second person needs to compile.
  • Two or more engineers are sharing one key today.
  • You want SSO instead of a passed-around invite link.
  • You need an audit log of who compiled what when.
Teams → Business
When more than one team compiles in the same org.
  • Two or more product teams need their own registry scopes.
  • You want SCIM provisioning and Okta, not Google SSO alone.
  • You need a webhook bridge to your CI/CD platform.
Business → Enterprise
When legal or your auditor enters the conversation.
  • BAA / DPA / DPIA are non-negotiable for the deployment.
  • Compile pipeline must run inside a customer-controlled VPC.
  • Receipts need external anchoring or long-horizon retention.

The pricing page exists so you can answer one question: Which threshold are you at right now?

-FAQ

The questions buyers actually ask.

If yours isn't here, ask in GitHub Discussions or mail dev@kolm.ai.

Do I need to understand LoRA or distillation?

No. You give examples and a task description. kolm handles the rest.

Who pays for the model API calls?

You do. kolm uses your key only during compile. We never resell tokens, never mark up frontier inference, never bill you for usage we didn't run on our infrastructure.

One current compile pass builds and signs a recipe-mode artifact with eval and receipt evidence. LoRA training, populated recall indexes, and byte-identical model rebuilds are bridge targets until shipped.

What if K-score is below the gate?

The artifact does not ship. You see the breakdown by component, can adjust seed examples or evals, and recompile. We don't charge for sub-gate compiles on Pro and Enterprise.

Recipe-mode artifacts are small signed zips. Model-backed artifacts will depend on base weights, adapter size, and recall index size once those bridges ship and publish benchmarks.

20-200 MB. The base is a 1B-7B open-weight INT4 quant. The LoRA is 20-200 MB. The recall index scales with corpus size; we shard above 4 GB.

Recipe-mode artifacts can run through the current CLI after download. Detached model execution requires the native/runtime bridge and deployment verification.

Provider support depends on the configured hosted deployment or customer bridge. Treat each teacher integration as a supported target only after it is enabled and tested.

Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Mistral, Together, Fireworks, AWS Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, your own gateway. Any HTTP completion endpoint that follows the OpenAI or Messages contract.

Open-weight base selection is the model bridge target. Current public artifacts record a base-model pointer rather than bundling verified model weights.

Qwen 2.5, Qwen 3.x, Llama 3.x, Phi-3, Hermes 3, Gemma 2, at INT4, sized 1B to 14B. Enterprise tier supports private base models you provision.

Trial policy is not guaranteed in the public product. Check the active sales agreement or contact dev@kolm.ai before relying on trial terms.

Yes. The first 14 days of Pro are free; cancel any time. Artifacts you compiled during trial stay yours and remain runnable forever.

You can export the signed artifact and receipt evidence the current product emits. Recall-index export and full cloud independence depend on the bridge and storage model in the deployment.

Yes. The artifact is a signed zip; you already own it. The recall index exports as Parquet. Receipts export as JSONL. Nothing is locked behind our cloud.

Do you offer non-profit or academic pricing?

Yes. Pro is free for verified non-profits and accredited academic researchers. Mail us with a .edu or .org address and we'll set it up.
