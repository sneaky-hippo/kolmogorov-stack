Common questions

Do I pay for model tokens through kolm?

No. Compile uses your Anthropic / OpenAI / Together / Moonshot key directly. Your model bill goes to them; your kolm bill is just the compile + recall + registry hosting.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Stripe portal, one click, no retention prompts. All your .kolm artifacts remain yours and keep running on every device they're already installed on, even after cancellation.

Where does the data live?

Recall vectors are encrypted at rest in our managed index by default. Free / Mobile / Pro tiers can opt in to on-device-only via a self-hosted sqlite-vec backend. Enterprise can run the full stack on-prem.

What's a "compile"?

One kolm compile turns a task description plus optional examples and corpus into one signed .kolm artifact. Re-compiling the same task with new data does not count against your monthly limit until the K-score crosses the publish threshold.
