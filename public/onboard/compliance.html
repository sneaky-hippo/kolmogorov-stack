Drift and supersession

A frozen artifact does not stay correct forever. kolm builds the lifecycle ladder into the spec itself, with four named tiers from stale notification through forced supersession.

M+1 stale notification

Surfaced 90 days post-freeze. No action required; the notice lands on /dashboard.

M+2 re-distill cadence

Quarterly by default. Configurable per spec. Emits a new artifact and supersession block.

M+3 drift detection

Cron-driven. Compares to tenant shadow corpus. Two tolerance bands (warn, breach).

M+4 supersession chain

Six named reasons. Linked artifacts. Auditor verifies the chain end to end via Rekor.
