Audit trail

Every operation. Receipted.

Every kolm action that touches your data leaves an entry. Captures, label exports, distill jobs, artifact signings. Per-tenant, queryable in JSON or CSV, signed by the same HMAC chain that signs your .kolm artifacts. The audit log is the receipt; legal can ask for it directly.

The receipt field is an HMAC over the entry under a per-tenant key derived from your RECIPE_RECEIPT_SECRET. Tampering with any field breaks the chain; kolm verify-receipt rejects.

What we don't log

  • Your upstream API key. The x-upstream-api-key header is forwarded once and dropped. Never persisted.
  • Your full prompt or response text. We hash for clustering and dedupe; the raw text lives in observations only if you opt in (and is yours to delete via /v1/observations/purge).
  • Cross-tenant data. Your audit log is scoped to your tenant id. There is no path that returns another tenant's entries.

Honest disclosure

The /v1/audit/log endpoint is in beta and returns 503 on tenants that haven't opted in. Opt in via POST /v1/account/audit-log {"enabled": true}. The opt-in itself is logged. Once enabled the log is durable; rotation and tenancy migration both preserve receipt chains.
