03 · The deal

What you get. What we ask for.

We do not pretend this is altruism. You give us your design feedback; we give you the product, the team, and a public win. Both sides should leave better off.

we give

Six things, every partner.

  • Free Pro tier for 12 months: unlimited compiles, 10M-vector recall.
  • Direct Slack channel with the engineers building kolm. No CSM layer.
  • Engineer-on-call for your first three production artifacts. We will pair on the verifier and the eval suite.
  • Roadmap influence. Design partners vote on the next quarter’s shipped features.
  • Logo placement on the homepage and a co-bylined case study, on your timeline, with your edits.
  • Soft pricing lock: if you choose to stay on Pro or upgrade to Business, you keep your design-partner rate for 24 months past GA.
we ask

Three things, in exchange.

  • One real workflow compiled into a .kolm in the first 30 days. Real data, real eval set, real users.
  • One 30-minute call per month with a kolm engineer to review the artifacts you shipped, the bugs you hit, and the things that almost worked.
  • Permission to publish (one case study, one quote, one logo) once the artifact has been in production for 60 days. You see and approve every word before it goes live.
