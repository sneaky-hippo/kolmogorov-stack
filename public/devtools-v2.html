01The pain

Why the AI feature
blocked the GA cut.

Three failure modes platform teams at dev-tool companies hit when their AI feature scales past pilot.

Gross margin

Per-token cost eats the gross margin.

At seat-priced SaaS economics, a frontier-API call per active session inverts the margin model. kolm artifacts run on hardware you already pay for; marginal cost approaches zero.

Rate limit

Provider rate limits gate your TPS.

A burst from one large customer can starve the rest of the tenant pool. kolm artifacts scale with your own infra. No shared upstream quota.

Privacy

Your customers do not want their code in someone else's training set.

Enterprise buyers ask the question on the security review every time. kolm runs locally; nothing is sent upstream. Make the answer one word.
