What you can do
  • Refresh — it might just be a transient glitch.
  • Check /status. The synthesis backend, runtime, and registry each have their own pulse.
  • If a Recipe-call failed: pass use_cache=false to bypass any bad cache entry, or call /v1/recipes/<id> to confirm it still exists.
  • If it persists: file an issue and we'll dig in.
