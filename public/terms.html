Every .kolm file you compile is owned by you. You can copy it, run it, ship it inside your product, or sell it. You can delete it at will. We claim no ownership over your artifacts.

05Frontier model bills

The compile pipeline calls the frontier model you bring. You pay for those calls directly to the frontier provider. We do not mark up, rebill, or front the frontier API spend.

We may disable accounts that violate these. We will tell you when we do, with reason.
