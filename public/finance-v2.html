01The pain

Why the model risk team
blocked the last vendor.

Three failure modes that surface in MRM and second-line review on the first frontier-API procurement.

BSA / AML

SAR narratives cannot leave the bank.

31 CFR 1020.320 confidentiality covers the SAR and any document that would reveal its existence. kolm runs entirely inside the bank network. No third party at inference.

KYC / CIP

Customer identification data is restricted.

SSN, DOB, source-of-funds attestations cannot be passed to an unauthorized processor. kolm redacts before capture and pins the redaction policy into the artifact.

SR 11-7

Model risk needs a reproducible eval.

Frontier APIs change without notice; MRM cannot sign off on a moving target. kolm freezes the model and ships a K-score eval bundle every recompile.
